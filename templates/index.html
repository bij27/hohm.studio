Yoga Reduces Stress & Anxiety

A 2024 systematic review found that yoga significantly reduces stress levels in the general population. Practitioners reported lower anxiety, improved well-being, and better sleep quality after regular practice.

Source: Frontiers in Psychiatry, 2024

Better Posture, Better Mood

Research shows good posture improves mood, energy levels, and self-confidence. Proper alignment reduces fatigue and allows for better breathing and circulation.

Source: Psychology Today, Harvard Health

Relief from Chronic Pain

Back pain costs over $12 billion annually in healthcare. Studies show that yoga and posture correction can significantly reduce chronic pain without medication.

Source: American Heart Association, NIH

Increased Productivity

Good posture improves blood flow to the brain, enhancing cognitive function and focus. Workers with better posture report higher productivity and less fatigue.

Source: Mayo Clinic
